# Senior Backend Engineer (Platform) — NimbusPay

## About the role

We're building the payments platform that powers NimbusPay's products. You'll
work on high-throughput, low-latency services and help shape our move toward a
more resilient, observable platform. This is a hands-on senior role with strong
influence over architecture.

## What you'll do

- Design, build, and operate distributed backend services for payment
  processing at high scale.
- Improve reliability, observability, and performance of critical services.
- Collaborate with product and platform teams on API design.
- Participate in an on-call rotation and drive incident reviews.
- Mentor mid-level engineers and raise the engineering bar.

## Must-have requirements

- 5+ years of backend engineering experience.
- Strong experience with at least one of: Go, Java, or Rust.
- Production experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration.
- Deep understanding of distributed systems (consistency, queues, idempotency).
- Experience with relational databases and data modeling at scale.
- Solid grounding in observability (metrics, logging, tracing).

## Nice to have

- Experience in fintech or payments.
- Experience with event-driven architectures and message brokers (Kafka).
- Exposure to infrastructure-as-code (Terraform).
- Track record of mentoring and technical leadership.

## What we offer

- Remote-friendly within the EU.
- Meaningful equity and a strong engineering culture.
